Gambitor - the national school championship. After achieving a global reach and surpassing 5000+ registrations from more than 20 states, we’re back again, with even more excitement in store for you. Venture with us into the cosmic realms, with an exhilarating theme of Pokemon in Space. Prepare to embark on an adventure where victory awaits those with exceptional creativity and quick thinking. Engage in a comprehensive exam crafted by our student community, which will push the boundaries of your logical and analytical skills. Test your mettle against the brightest minds of the nation in a bid to become the greatest in the cosmos.

The exam is going to be conducted in TWO ROUNDS

INCEPTION ROUND

For the first round of the GambitoR, battle it out with your fellow Pokemon Masters online. Wrestle a 2-hour question paper consisting of math, puzzles, code crunchers etc., and put your logical skills, guile and perception up for a challenge. The top 20 students from the 9th & 10th and 20 students from 11th grade category and top 15 students from 12th grade category will get to visit IIT Roorkee for the second round, ‘Pinnacle’ and win exciting prizes and certificates.

PINNACLE ROUND

Once you’ve made it through, an exciting visit to the IITR campus awaits you. Taking in the beauty and extravagance of our campus, and adding that to the excitement of the various technical events for the second round, it will be a glorious and exhilarating experience. Push your limits, gauge your potential, and have fun while exploring the ins and outs of the campus.The top performers in the events will be awarded cash prizes and certificates of excellence
